Winter sausage recipe

Items needed

Ingredients:

  • 7 pounds of pork
  • 1.5 cup cranberries
  • half clove of garlic
  • 5 tsp black pepper
  • 5 tsp salt
  • 2.25 Tbsp fresh sage
  • 1/2 tsp of nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp of clove
  • 1/8 tsp of cinnamon
  • sausage casings and ties

Equipment and supplies:

  • meat grinder with sausage filler attachment
  • large knife (cleaver or chef's knife)
  • a few bowls
  • butcher's twine

Procedure

The following is best done with two or more people—one to feed the pork into the grinder and the other to feed the grinder's output into the casings.

  1. Crush and chop cranberries and set aside.
  2. Mix all spices together in a bowl and set aside.
  3. Chop pork into small pieces with knife and place in large bowl. Keep the bowl as cold as possible.
  4. Feed chopped meat into grinder and collect ground output into another bowl.
  5. Thoroughly mix ground meat, cranberries, and spices together in large bowl.
  6. Attach the sausage filler attachment to the grinder and place a casing on the end.
  7. Turn on the grinder's motor or begin rotating it by hand if using a manual one.
  8. Begin feeding the sausage mix into the grinder.
  9. Every seven inches or so, twist the casing 2–3 revolutions and tie off with butcher's twine to create a new link.
  10. Continue until the casing is filled and tie off the end.
  11. Repeat steps 8–10 as necessary to fill up casings until the pork is gone.

Freeze raw and store in freezer for up to three months.
